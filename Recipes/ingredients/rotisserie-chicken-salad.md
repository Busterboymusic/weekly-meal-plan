# Rotisserie Chicken Salad
**Rotation:** Week 3 | **Protein:** Chicken (Costco rotisserie) | **Method:** No cook | **Time:** 15 min

## Ingredients (per serving)
| Ingredient | Qty (1 serving) | Qty (3) | Qty (5) | Category | Store |
|-----------|----------------|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Costco rotisserie chicken | 1 cup shredded | 1 whole chicken | 1 whole chicken + extra | Prepared | Costco |
| Mayonnaise | 1.5 tbsp | 1/4 cup | 1/3 cup | Condiment | *staple* |
| Celery, diced | 1 stalk | 3 stalks | 5 stalks | Produce | Safeway |
| Canned water chestnuts | 2 tbsp | 1 can (8 oz) | 1 can (8 oz) | Pantry | Walmart |
| Dijon mustard | 1 tsp | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | Condiment | *staple* |
| Lemon juice | 1 tsp | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | Produce | Safeway |
| Salt & pepper | to taste | — | — | Pantry | *staple* |
| Lettuce leaves (for wraps) | 2-3 leaves | 1 head | 1.5 heads | Produce | Safeway |

## Notes
- Simple version: no grapes, no nuts
- Served as lettuce wraps
- Shop Costco same day for fresh rotisserie chicken
- Canned water chestnuts for crunch
