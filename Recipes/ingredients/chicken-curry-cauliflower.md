# Chicken Curry with Cauliflower
**Rotation:** Week 2 | **Protein:** Chicken | **Method:** Stovetop | **Time:** 30 min

| Ingredient | Qty (1 serving) | Qty (3) | Qty (5) | Category | Store |
|-----------|----------------|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Chicken breasts | 1 breast | 3 breasts | 5 breasts | Meat | Costco |
| Cauliflower | 1/3 head | 1 head | 1.5 heads | Produce | Safeway |
| Mayonnaise | 2/3 cup | 2 cups | 3 cups | Condiment | *staple* |
| Plain yogurt | 2/3 cup | 2 cups | 3 cups | Dairy | Safeway |
| Onion, diced | 1/3 onion | 1 onion | 1.5 onions | Produce | *staple* |
| Paprika | to taste | — | — | Pantry | *staple* |
| Pepper | to taste | — | — | Pantry | *staple* |
| Curry powder | to taste | — | — | Pantry | *staple* |
| Ginger powder | to taste | — | — | Pantry | *staple* |

## Notes
- Recipe base is for 3 servings (3 chicken breasts, 1 head cauliflower)
- Mayo and yogurt create the curry sauce — no coconut milk
