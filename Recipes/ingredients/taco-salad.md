# Taco Salad
**Rotation:** Week 3 | **Protein:** Beef | **Method:** Stovetop | **Time:** 20 min

## Ingredients (per serving)
| Ingredient | Qty (1 serving) | Qty (3) | Qty (5) | Category | Store |
|-----------|----------------|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Ground beef | 5 oz | 1 lb | 1.5 lbs | Meat | Safeway |
| Taco seasoning | 1 tbsp | 3 tbsp (1 packet) | 5 tbsp | Pantry | Walmart |
| Romaine lettuce, chopped | 2 cups | 1 head | 1.5 heads | Produce | Safeway |
| Tomato, diced | 1/4 cup | 1 medium | 2 medium | Produce | Safeway |
| Shredded cheese (Mexican blend) | 1/4 cup | 3/4 cup | 1 cup | Dairy | Safeway |
| Sour cream | 2 tbsp | 1/3 cup | 1/2 cup | Dairy | Safeway |
| Avocado or guacamole | 1/4 avocado | 1 avocado | 2 avocados | Produce | Safeway |
| Black beans (optional) | 2 tbsp | 1/4 can | 1/2 can | Pantry | Walmart |
| Salsa | 2 tbsp | 1/3 cup | 1/2 cup | Condiment | Walmart |
| Pickled jalapenos | 1 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 5 tbsp | Condiment | Walmart |
| Tortilla strips or crushed chips (optional, small amount) | 1 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 5 tbsp | Pantry | Walmart |
| Red onion, diced | 1 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 1/4 cup | Produce | *staple* |

## Notes
- Keep ingredients separate for leftovers — especially ground beef
- Cook extra ground beef when leftovers are wanted
